1 Datasets

1.1 go_eden_pelagie_2013_07_01.dta

Merge of Eden and Pelagie datasets realised by Otana for her project in when?. This dataset will probably be lacking some variables as is was not produced for this study and there have been some modifications since this merge concerning PELAGIE:

“Depuis 2013, la base Pélagie a été corrigée pour la variable du sexe de l’enfant. Sur votre base, les changements concernent 24 enfants. Ces corrections impactent la génération de la variable SGA” Emie 22/05/2020

Contains outcome and most covariates. Not the exposures.

1.2 bdd_grossesse_v4.dta

Latest version of the sepages pregnancy dataset. should contain all sepages outcomes and covariates. Not exposures.

3.3 Delivery

Sepages:

  • po_delmod [numeric] Delivery mode (2 categories 1: vaginal/2: c-section)
  • cy1hao1_q07 Début travail (spontané/déclenché/césarienne avt travail)
  • cy1hao1_q10 Naissance par (voie basse non instrumentale/extraction voie basse instrumentale/césarienne programmée/césarienne en urgence)

Eden pelagie:

  • go_modetra [haven_labelled] mode debut travail (spontane/declenche ou césarienne avt travail)
  • go_modeacc [haven_labelled] mode accouchement (voie basse/césarienne avt travail/césarienne pdt travail)

3.5 Tobacco

Sepages tobacco vars:

  • mo_tob_avgr [numeric] Maternal smoking before pregnancy, cig./day
  • mo_tob_grstt1 [numeric] Maternal smoking between preg. conception and detection, cig./day
  • mo_tob_grstt1_yn [numeric] Maternal smoking between preg. conception and detection, N/Y
  • mo_tob_grstt1_yn1 [numeric] Maternal smoking between preg. conception and detection above 1 cig./day, N/Y
  • mo_tob_grt1 [numeric] Maternal smoking during 1st trim of pregnancy, after diagnosis, cig./day
  • mo_tob_grt1_yn [numeric] Maternal smoking during 1st trim of pregnancy, after diagnosis, N/Y
  • mo_tob_grt1_yn1 [numeric] Maternal smoking during 1st trim of pregnancy, after diagnosis, above 1 cig./day
  • mo_tob_grt2 [numeric] Maternal smoking during 2nd trim of pregnancy, cig./day Mean (sd)
  • mo_tob_grt2_yn [numeric] Maternal smoking during 2nd trim of pregnancy, N/Y
  • mo_tob_grt2_yn1 [numeric] Maternal smoking during 2nd trim of pregnancy above 1 cig./day, N/Y
  • mo_tob_grt3 [numeric] Maternal smoking during 3rd trim of pregnancy, cig./day
  • mo_tob_grt3_yn [numeric] Maternal smoking during 3rd trim of pregnancy, N/Y
  • mo_tob_grt3_yn1 [numeric] Maternal smoking during 3rd trim of pregnancy above 1 cig./day, N/Y
  • mo_tob_gr_anyt_yn [numeric] Maternal smoking during any trim of diagnosed pregnancy, N/Y
  • mo_tob_gr_anyt_yn1 [numeric] Maternal smoking during any trim of diagnosed pregnancy above 1 cig./day, N/Y

All Eden - Pelagie tobacco vars:

  • go_tabac [numeric] nb cig/jr trim1 pour EDEN, inclusion pour PELAGIE
  • go_tabaccl [haven_labelled] nb cig/jr en classes trim1 pour EDEN, inclusion pour PELAGIE
  • go_tabapass [haven_labelled] tabagisme passif de la mère

3.7 Health issues

  • Sepages first general question:
    • mt1haa1_q40 = AT T1: “depuis le début de votre grossesse, est-ce qu’un médecin ou une sage-femme vous a diagnostiqué des complications liées à votre grossesse ?”
  • Hypertension gravidique
    • Sepages =
      • mt1haa1_q40p1 = Hypertension à T1 (see mt1haa1_q40 first)
      • mt1haa1_q40p2 = Nombre de SA Hypertension à T1 (see mt1haa1_q40 first)
      • mt3haf1_q01 = At T3: “Depuis le début de votre grossesse, vous a-t-on diagnostiqué de l’hypertension?”
      • mt3haf1_q01p1 = Nombre SA Hypertension (see mt3haf1_q01 first)
    • Eden - Pelagie = go_htg
  • Gestational diabetes
    • Sepages =
      • mt1haa1_q40p7 = diabète gestationnel T1 (see mt1haa1_q40 first)
      • mt1haa1_q40p8 = nombre SA diabète gestationnel T1 (see mt1haa1_q40 first)
      • mt3haf1_q04 = At T3: “Depuis le début de votre grossesse, vous a-t-on diagnostiqué du diabète gestationnel?”
      • mt3haf1_q04p1 = Nombre SA diabète gestationnel (see mt3haf1_q04 first)
    • Eden - Pelagie = go_diabges
